A singular, discreet, and practiced firm

All Advisors on the Deans of Admissions team are former admissions executives with senior decision-making responsibilities at highly selective universities' admissions offices. Our team includes former admissions officers from Harvard, Oxford, Cambridge, Princeton, Columbia, the University of Pennsylvania, Yale, Brown, Dartmouth, Cornell, MIT, Stanford, and the Juilliard School.

Founded by a former Dean of Admissions

We are the only college admissions advisory firm founded by the former Dean of Admissions of an Ivy League university. Dean D. Blackman, in her role as Dean of Admissions at both Harvard and Columbia, created the contemporary framework for highly selective admissions. Many of her protégés are now current deans of admissions across the country.

Since our founding more than two decades ago, 100% of students who began our counseling services in the 8th grade (or equivalent) and met our GPA and SAT/ACT targets have been admitted to at least one of their top-choice universities.

Our clients

Our clients include royal families, single-family offices, public figures, entrepreneurs, Fortune 500 executives, technologists, investors, and philanthropists. Our clients value discretion. Therefore, we neither request nor display references.
